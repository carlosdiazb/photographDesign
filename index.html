ABOUT US
I'm a visual artist and fine art photographer from Spain, focused on atmospheric and abstract landscape photography. My approach is all about slow, conscious travel. A truly stunning landscape photo takes patience.My focus is on capturing remote, rarely seen places and perspectives.

All things considered, I started my vocation as an expert on-screen character, so I think about long shots. What’s more, photography truly occurred by possibility.
DOCUMENTARY
One of my services is to make photographs to all types of documentaries, I am specialized about doing documentaries photos of the nature.
COVERAGE
Another area in which I work is reporting all kinds of events, both during their course and before it. Some of them are weddings, communions, ... etc.
I usually spend several weeks at each location, speaking with locals and discovering the small, hidden spots and unique stories. I’m truly fascinated by the periphery, places where people have settled in seemingly impenetrable landscapes.The vast expanse, dotted by remnants of civilization.

My personal projects are a visual journal. These moments of quiet contemplation are where some of my most distinctive work originates.
LANDSCAPES
Here comes my favorite part of my work and the reason why I started with photography and they are landscapes and everything related to nature.

Without glazing, a photo print on Aluminium Dibond has a very purist and timeless look. Aluminium Dibond is a 3 mm thick composite material with three layers. Two white coated aluminium plates enclose a polyethylene core in black colour, which can also be seen from the side. The material is extremely robust and at the same time light in weight, making it ideal for large-format parts. The high-quality photo print is laminated with a very thin film for protection and applied to the aluminium dibond without any bubbles.
